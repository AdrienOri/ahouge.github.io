I am a postdoctoral researcher working at the GLOBE Institute at the University of Copenhagen. My research focuses on the early stages of planet formation in discs around young stars. Basically, I spend my days coding the coagulation and transport of little dust grains to constrain where planets form, and what material they are made of - who knows, maybe the right stuff to make Life?

I have a deep passion for graphic design and building awesome schematics for my science. Make sure to check them out in the relevant section of my website!

Aside from science, I love hiking, cooking, ice swimming, traveling by train on insanely long distances, collecting dusty indie records, and all sorts of other ridiculous things.
